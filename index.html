Rules Snapshot

  • Tyler: 5 cards down per player, 5 community pairs down, reveal one pair after each betting round.
  • Must use exactly 3 cards from hand and 2 community cards to make a 5-card hand.
  • High/Low split. Low uses natural cards only; wilds cannot create a low.
  • Follow the Queen: a face-up queen makes the next face-up card wild. If the last face-up card is a queen, queens are wild.
  • If betting on the last round and the game has a burn, losing after the final bet owes half the pot or $2, whichever is lower.
  • Betting caps: max bet $0.25, max raise $0.25, and at most 3 raises per betting round. No all-ins.
